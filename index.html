AI That Works for Every CVS Team

For a healthcare and retail leader operating at the scale of roughly a billion web clicks a week, Amplitude turns fragmented behavioral data into trustworthy, AI-ready insight your business partners can actually act on.

The Challenge

CVS's digital and analytics teams have a clear top priority: a step-change in how they deliver trustworthy insights to business partners—balancing deterministic truth with AI-driven intelligence without losing credibility. Today, web is the big bet at roughly a billion clicks a week, all captured in Snowflake and BigQuery.

But without curated, semantic layers on top of that raw event data, teams are stuck in ad-hoc analysis and dependent on specialists for every question—producing slower answers, inconsistent numbers, and eroding trust with the business partners who rely on them.

How Amplitude Helps
  • A trusted behavioral layer — time-ordered customer journeys and a governed taxonomy that turn ~1B weekly web events into "official," deterministic charts every team can rely on.
  • AI that never stops working — a Global Agent plus purpose-built agents that continuously monitor product data and generate insights, so answers no longer wait on the data team.
  • Insight to action in one platform — experimentation, surveys, in-product guides, and cohort delivery to downstream channels, mapping to CVS's doc-summarization, process-automation, and data-insight AI themes.
  • Built for healthcare scale — proven with McKesson, Moderna, and Amazon Pharmacy, and integrated with Snowflake and BigQuery to meet CVS's data, security, and compliance needs.
